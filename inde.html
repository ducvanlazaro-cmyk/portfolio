Penguin Dataset — Tableau Data Visualization Report

Using Tableau, I visualized the Palmer Penguins dataset across four chart types: a body mass histogram by species and sex, a population line chart over time, a bar chart of bill length by island, and a scatter plot of bill length vs. body mass. Each visualization is paired with a written reflection exploring what the data reveals about identity, ecology, and what gets captured — or erased — in datasets.
